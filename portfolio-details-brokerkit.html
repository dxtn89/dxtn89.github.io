Brokerkit

Project information

  • Project URL: brokerkit.com
  • Client: Providing staff augmentation services and delivering custom software development solutions to startups, SMBs, and enterprises.

Our client aimed to speed up time-to-market by adding high-seniority engineers who strengthen the in-house team. The main challenge was to build an app that would satisfy all the needs and expectations of real-estate brokers. The client put the real-life experience of offline agent recruiting, onboarding and retention in the first place.The main focus was on making the platform that makes routine processes of brokers and real estate agents faster and easier.

While our team was dedicated to the development process, we worked on implementation of following features: multi-user configurations, admin panel with user, role & access management, platform integrations with APIs, and development of some modules for the recruiting CRM. The solution is currently available for the US market.

Cooperation : 2019 - 2020

Project highlights :
  • Custom-made recruiting CRM and admin panel for users & content management to ensure rapid data processing as well as simplify user journey map.
  • Leads segmentation and import from various data sources, outreach automation.
  • Development of agents recruiting, onboarding, and retention management system that allows brokers to streamline hiring processes.
